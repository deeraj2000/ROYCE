rols-Royce-phantom

Rolls-Royce has revealed the prices of the eighth-generation of the Phantom in India. The CBU unit is priced at Rs 9.5 crores for the standard wheelbase and extends to Rs 11.5 crores for the extended wheelbase version with personalisation options.


Rolls-Royce-Ghost

Priced at Rs 4.7 crore, the Rolls-Royce Ghost is among the most affordable luxury cars in the Rolls-Royce range. But that doesn’t make it any less of a status symbol. If anything, it’s more expensive than the Mercedes S-Class and Bentley Flying Spur by about Rs 2 crore.
